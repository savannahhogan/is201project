Hunt for the Wilderpeople - Taikia Watiti

A MOVIE REVIEW

Posted by Savannah Hogan on April 13, 2020

What an absolute TREAT! My family was maybe a little hesitant to watch this - after my sister dubbed it a "hippie movie" - but we were all very impressed.

Hunt for the Wilderpeople is the story of Ricky Baker, a foster child from the city, who moves in with a new family on the edge of the New Zealand bush. Ricky grows to love his new family and environment, but his new foster dad isn't exactly friendly. When an unfortunate tragedy forces them into the bush, Ricky and his "Uncle" learn how to survive on the knack alone.

Not only is this movie incredibly heartwarming, IT IS HILARIOUS. I wasn't expecting it to be funny! I wasn't ready! The humor is well dispersed throughout the film, making it an easy watch.

"Trees. Birds. Rivers. Sky. / Running with my Uncle Hec / Living forever." - Ricky Baker

I am so happy that I was convinced to watch this movie I knew absolutely nothing about, because now I have a new favorite.
